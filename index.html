The Goal of This Site

The Oman FPV Community website aims to serve as a central hub for FPV drone enthusiasts, providing valuable guides, tips, and content to both beginners and experienced pilots.
My mission is to promote the sport of FPV racing and drone flying in Oman and beyond, encouraging more people to join this exciting and innovative hobby.

Additionally, through this platform, I hope to expand the FPV business, offering resources and support to help individuals get started with FPV drones and build a strong community around this growing sport.
